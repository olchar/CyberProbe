🎣 Phishing Campaign Investigation Graph

End-to-end investigation workflow for phishing campaigns — from initial alert through email analysis, URL/attachment detonation, credential compromise detection, post-compromise activity hunting, and containment/remediation.

  • Phase 1 — Alert Triage: Classify the phishing alert and extract initial IOCs
  • Phase 2 — Email Analysis: Identify all recipients, delivery actions, and campaign scope
  • Phase 3 — URL & Attachment Analysis: Detonate URLs, analyze payloads, check SafeLinks clicks
  • Phase 4 — Identity Compromise: Check for credential harvesting, suspicious sign-ins, MFA bypass
  • Phase 5 — Post-Compromise Hunting: Inbox rules, lateral phishing, data exfiltration, persistence
  • Phase 6 — Containment & Remediation: Block IOCs, revoke sessions, purge emails, generate report
